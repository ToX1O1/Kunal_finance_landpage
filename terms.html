How Fraudsters May Try to Trick You (Phishing/Malware)

  • Phishing: Fake emails appearing to be sent by a credible source to compel you to reveal personal information.
  • Vishing: Phone calls by people pretending to work for Grow Geniuss or another legitimate business, asking you to reveal personal information.
  • Trojan / Virus: Programs inadvertently installed on your computer to steal sensitive information or spy on you.
  • Ransomware: Malware that infects your computer, blocks access to files, and demands money to regain access.
  • Spyware: Software that secretly monitors activity on your computer and reports back to the fraudster.

  • Memorize your passwords instead of writing them down, and don‚Äôt share them with anybody.
